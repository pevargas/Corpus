    About

    As a software engineering student, I enjoy programming robust programs that help people. The University of Colorado Boulder, as well as my internship with Pitney Bowes Software, has fully prepared me to take on the task of creating object-oriented software in an agile development environment. As a generalist, my multiple passions lead me to exciting areas of computer science. In my free time, as well as side careers, I work as a full stack developer, from php and mysql, to jquery and css3. In addition, as an Atmospheric and Oceanic Science minor, and Technology, Arts and Media minor, I feel I can bring a diverse background to whatever job is at hand. My leadership and involvement experience help to bring this diverse background to the forefront of whatever task is at hand.
