    About

    Passionate

    As a software engineer, I enjoy programming robust programs that help people. The University of Colorado Boulder, as well as my internship with Pitney Bowes Software, has fully prepared me to take on the task of creating object-oriented software in an agile development environment. My leadership and involvement experience help to bring this diverse background to the forefront of whatever task is at hand.

    Holistic

    As a generalist, my multiple passions lead me to exciting areas of computer science. In my free time, as well as side careers, I work as a full stack developer, from grails and the lamp stack, to jquery and css3. I love to learn new languages and new technologies. This drives my passion for software engineering. With the polyglot skills I've picked up during my time at CU, I feel very confident to learn whatever new technology comes my way. In addition, as an Atmospheric and Oceanic Science minor, and a Technology, Arts and Media minor, I feel I can bring a diverse background to whatever job I am in.

    Enthusiastic

    Software engineering is my calling. I am optimistic about where technology is leading our society. Each day, I read the news of what's happening. Technology is definitely a great place to work as it can help people in every aspect of their daily lives.
