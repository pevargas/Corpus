This I believe

Maybe I'm a little off topic. A couple years ago, I believed in the power of self motivation. I recognized within myself that my path to happiness is in my hands. Society owes me nothing. It is up to me to find what makes me happy.

Sophomore year, I took my destiny in my own hands and lost 50 pounds simply by exercising. Junior year, I tried adding weight lifting into my routine. This lead me to develop some definition and helped to gain self confidence.

Then was the moment of truth. All this lead to my first date in November of 2011. Then a second date. And a third. Granted, they weren't the same guy, but the fact of the matter was I finally had a date. I no longer felt melancholic and jealous of my queer peers. If I wanted a date, I had to take charge and make it happen.

I continue to this day in my pursuit of love. My new years resolution is to run the Bolder Boulder. My thought is by training for the BB plus continuing my diet plan, I can finally lose enough weight to fence off my bad body image. I have faith in myself to obtain this goal. I believe in myself to find a man to have my first relationship with. I can do this. I believe in myself.
